Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa application process take?
Processing times vary by visa type. Work visas typically take 1-3 months, while Permanent Residence can take 4-12 months. Our AI-enhanced approach helps expedite the process by ensuring accuracy from the start.
What is your success rate?
We maintain a 95%+ success rate across all visa categories with over 500 successful cases. Our AI system helps identify and address potential issues before submission.
Do I need to come to your office?
No. We handle most cases entirely online through secure video consultations and document sharing. In-person meetings are available if preferred.
What languages do you support?
We provide full support in English, Japanese, and Chinese. Our multilingual team and AI translation tools ensure clear communication throughout the process.
What happens if my application is rejected?
While rare, if an application is rejected, we'll analyze the reasons and advise on the best next steps. Our Premium plan includes a success guarantee with free resubmission.
How does AI improve your service?
Our AI tools review documents for completeness, generate supporting materials, identify potential issues, and ensure consistency. This reduces processing time by 60% while improving accuracy.
Can you help with visa extensions or changes?
Yes. We handle all types of visa changes and extensions, including changes in employment, residence, and status of residence adjustments.
