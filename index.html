Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa application process typically take? ▼
The processing time varies depending on the visa type and individual circumstances. With our AI-powered review system, we can complete initial document review and preparation in 48 hours. Immigration Bureau processing typically takes 2-4 weeks for standard applications, though complex cases may require additional time.
What makes your office different from other administrative scrivener offices? ▼
We are Japan's first AI-integrated administrative scrivener office, combining 15+ years of traditional legal expertise with advanced AI technology. This allows us to provide faster, more accurate service while maintaining the highest professional standards. We also offer comprehensive multilingual support and nationwide coverage.
Can you help with company establishment if I'm not currently in Japan? ▼
Yes, we can assist with remote company establishment procedures. We offer online consultations and can coordinate all necessary legal procedures on your behalf. Our multilingual team ensures smooth communication throughout the process, regardless of your location.
